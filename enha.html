more about enhypen!

Enhypen is a South Korean boy band formed by Belift Lab, a former joint venture between CJ ENM and Hybe Corporation, through the 2020 survival competition show I-Land. They have seven members known as Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. They debuteed on November 30, 2020, with their song "Given-Taken".
